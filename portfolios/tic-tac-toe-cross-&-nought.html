Tic Tac Toe: Cross & Nought

Project information

  • Category: Full Game Development
  • Client: Home Product (Konoha Games)
  • Project URL: Private
  • Game URL: Play Store
  • Description: This game is the extended version of the classic tic-tac-toe game. 3 new modes in addition to the classic mode for this game. First is matrix mode which is extended classic mode with 4x4, 5x5, and 6x6 grid. Second is surprise mode which is a bit of fun! In this mode, you won't have any particular sign. which sign you will have is the element of surprise. And third is quick Test in this try challenging yourself by defeating AI with less time than your last challenge. In addition to the 3 modes it has AI with the min-max algorithm, hooked animations, pleasing BGM & SFX, and creative UI/UX.
